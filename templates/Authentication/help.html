Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I create an event?

To create an event on ClubHub, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Events section.
  3. Click on the "Create Event" button.
  4. Fill in the event details and click "Submit."

2. Can I invite non-members to events?

Yes, you can invite non-members to events. During the event creation process, you can specify whether the event is open to non-members.

3. How do I manage club members?

To manage club members:

  1. Go to the Members section.
  2. Use the search and filter options to find specific members.
  3. Edit or remove members as needed.
